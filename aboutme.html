About Me

I’m a curious and creative design professional who cares deeply about finding innovative solutions to complicated problems. I work as a User Experience Designer for Veracode, an application security company. At work, I have plenty of opportunities to solve lots of interesting and many-layered design challenges, from designing new features, conducting usability testing and user feedback sessions, to the intricacies of being a shared resources across multiple scrum teams in a fast-paced agile environment.

I’m fascinated by people and information, and love that I have a job in which I am asked to help foster positive interactions between users and information.

Why I am a UX Designer

I’m a helplessly curious person with a strong creative bent. I’m deeply analytical and have always loved logic puzzles. I started out wanting to be an archivist. That work got me familiar with systems analysis and organizing huge swaths of data to provide access to patrons. I learned about UX design while I was in graduate school, and switched focus to an Human-Computer Interaction track when I realized I could do both systems analysis type work AND design! It seemed too good to be true! I’ve been happily practicing UX design ever since.
